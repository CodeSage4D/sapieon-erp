# AURXON ERP Lite — Institutional Onboarding Quick Start Guides

Welcome to AURXON ERP Lite! This document contains simplified, action-oriented checklists tailored specifically for school users. It is designed to get each role up and running immediately with zero technical jargon.

---

## 1. Principal & Administrator Guide (5-Minute Syllabus)

### Goal: Oversee school status, approve staff leaves, check data health, and monitor finances.

- [ ] **Step 1: Access the Cockpit Dashboard**
  - Sign in using your administrator credentials. Your home screen is the **Principal Cockpit**, structured as your daily Command Center.
- [ ] **Step 2: Check "What Needs Attention Today?"**
  - Scan the top row for high-priority alerts:
    - *Leaves Pending Review*: Active staff leave requests are displayed here. Click `[Approve]` or `[Reject]` to process them instantly.
    - *Document Security Check*: Verify if any student records are missing security signatures. Click `[Stamp Document Signatures]` to cryptographically secure all active files.
- [ ] **Step 3: Review Data Health Alerts**
  - Scroll down to check CBSE attendance and academic warnings. Scholars with average attendance &lt; 70% and marks &lt; 40% will be automatically flagged for your review. Click `[Notify Parents]` to dispatch circular warnings.
- [ ] **Step 4: Analyze Executive Insights**
  - Browse through the **Real-Time Executive Insights** panel. Switch between tabs (*Academic*, *Financial*, *Staff*, *Operational*) to view beautiful, live Recharts line and bar graphs mapping school performance trends.
  - Need to share these insights? Click `[Export PDF]` or `[Export Excel]` at the top of the analytics panel to download executive summaries.

## 2. Teacher Guide (2-Minute Syllabus)

### Goal: Mark daily student attendance, evaluate homework, and view class schedules.

- [ ] **Step 1: Access the Classroom Hub**
  - Log in using your teacher credentials. Your dashboard is the **Classroom Hub**, focused strictly on your daily teaching tasks.
- [ ] **Step 2: Take Daily Attendance (Roll-Call)**
  - Look at the top outstanding notice banner. If your daily roll-call is due, click `[Take Attendance Now]`.
  - The system will immediately redirect you to the attendance grid with your class roster pre-loaded. Check the boxes and click save.
- [ ] **Step 3: Evaluate Grading Queue**
  - Locate the **Homework Evaluation Queue** card. This shows student homework submissions awaiting grades.
  - Click `[Enter Grade]` directly on a student's card, input their score (e.g. 95/100), and click the checkmark. The score is immediately committed to their report cards.
- [ ] **Step 4: Track Class Schedule**
  - View the **Class Periods** card. This displays your timetable for today, highlighting completed, active, and upcoming subjects.

## 3. Parent Guide (1-Minute Syllabus)

### Goal: Track child standing, check report cards, and settle outstanding tuition fees.

- [ ] **Step 1: Access the Family Portal**
  - Log in using your parent credentials. Your home screen is the **Family Portal**, displaying a unified view of your child's standing.
- [ ] **Step 2: Review Attendance & Grades**
  - View the visual progress donuts showing your child's current attendance rate (e.g. 96.8%) and exam grade average.
  - Scroll down to review the detailed academic marksheet for recent terms.
- [ ] **Step 3: Settle Tuition Dues**
  - If there are outstanding dues, the **Outstanding Dues Ledger** will highlight a red overdue warning banner (e.g. ₹1,800 due).
  - Click `[Pay Tuition Dues Securely]`. Choose your payment method in the Stripe Sandbox, and click `[Authorize Payment Sandbox]`.
  - The dues are immediately cleared, and a digital receipt (`RCPT-2026-XXXXXX`) is generated.

## 4. Student Guide (1-Minute Syllabus)

### Goal: Check daily class schedules, submit homework worksheets, and view grades.

- [ ] **Step 1: Access the Academics Desk**
  - Log in using your student credentials. Your home screen is the **Academics Desk**, designed to keep your studies organized.
- [ ] **Step 2: Check Today's Timetable**
  - Scan the **Today's Class Schedule** timeline to see what subjects, classrooms, and teachers are scheduled next.
- [ ] **Step 3: Turn In Homework**
  - Locate the **Homework Assignments Due** card.
  - Select your active homework, click `[Turn In PDF]`, choose your completed worksheet file, and click `[Upload & Turn In]`. Your status will instantly update to `Submitted`.
